Our Story

Who Are We?

AgroMech Company is an online-based supplier of tractors and equipment in the agricultural industry. We are dedicated to providing first-class service to all of our customers, both home and abroad, using experienced haulers to ensure the highest quality service. AgroMech is registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission of the Federal Republic of Nigeria, under the Corporate and Allied Matters Act of 1990. AgroMech ensures that the clearing of machines, certificates and paper-works are completed to the highest standard
